Privacy Statement

What Data We Collect: We collect basic preferences that you set, such as your selected theme (light or dark) and, if provided, your display name. No personal or sensitive data is collected.

Why We Collect It: This information helps personalize your experience by remembering your settings between visits.

How Long Data Is Stored: All data is stored locally in your browser’s localStorage and automatically expires after 30 days. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Your Controls: You can clear your data anytime by clicking “Clear My Data” on any page. You can also choose not to provide optional information like your name. If we ever fetch external data, it will be clearly indicated.

We value your privacy and believe in giving you full control over the information stored on your device.
